Endless 3D Runner

A simple game made entirely out of javascript. The player uses the "wasd" and "space" keys for movement. The goal is to reach the end of the track, (yes I understand that hardly makes the game "endless") all while avoiding obsticles such as pillars, cross beams, and randomly positioned cubes.
